We observe that the images that contain more than one digit are misclassfied. Since our model is designed to classify single digits, it cannot classify multiple digits in a single image. When we observe the class of 5 we see that it is mostly being mistaken with 3. The quality of some of the misclassified images is extremely poor, even hard for humans to classify. However, the highest number of miss-classifications is between 6 and 0 and between 3 and 0. In general, 0 is often being misclassified as other digit.
