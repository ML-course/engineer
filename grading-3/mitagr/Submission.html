                Then MaxPooling was used which does two things. It increases the translation invariance and reduces the resolution, which are both necessary.

By running the model multiple times, I observed that the images which have more than one digit are the problem area and are usually misclassfied. 
                  Since the model I made is designed only to classify single digits (per image), it performs poorly when asked to classify multiple digits in a single image input. 
                  Another problematic area was classification of 8. Whenever the model encounters the class of 8, it is usually misclassified as 6 or 0. 
                  Some misclassified images were because of the extremely poor quality of images. These were even hard to classify by human eye.
                  Statistically, the highest number of missclassifications found was between 6 and 0 and between 3 and 0. 
                  Everything analyzed together, we can say that 0 is often being misclassified as other digit.
