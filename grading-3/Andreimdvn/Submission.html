Answer 3.1 (2p)¶

2 misclassified as 7

               2 is often classified as a 7. 
               Also 5 is often confused with 3, 8 or 9.
               Also, all numbers are often classified as 1.
               2 is often classified as 7 because these are very similar.
               Some of the presented images are blurry and others have the 2 very close
               to the end of the image and this information can be lost in the network.
